# Chess Piece Images

Place your chess piece images in this directory with the following naming convention:

## Required Files (12 total):

### White Pieces:
- `wk.png` - White King
- `wq.png` - White Queen
- `wr.png` - White Rook
- `wb.png` - White Bishop
- `wn.png` - White Knight
- `wp.png` - White Pawn

### Black Pieces:
- `bk.png` - Black King
- `bq.png` - Black Queen
- `br.png` - Black Rook
- `bb.png` - Black Bishop
- `bn.png` - Black Knight
- `bp.png` - Black Pawn

## Recommended Specs:
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Size: 256x256px or similar square dimensions
